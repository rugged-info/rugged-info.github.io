This is a coordination site we developed to make sure our employees and our customers have access to important information about projects.

Customers

If you are a new customer who would like to begin recording materials and design decisions for your renovation please click on Forms in the main menu. Then choose the appropriate form for your renovation - either kitchen or bathroom. Ignore the forms which say “Demo”. These will be filled out by James or one of the project leads.

Please note that if you have already submitted a form and you would like to add or make changes, follow the instructions that were included in the confirmation email you received the last time you submitted. That will be easier than submitting a brand new form.

Employees and Customers

If you would like to review the project summary which includes demo instructions, design and materials decisions please go to the Projects page found in the main menu and type the last name of the customer into the white box. Then click submit.

Please note that our data entry process is not fully automated, so information entered into forms may take up to 24 hours to be reflected in the project’s summary page.
